work # 2/3 of my artistic research PhD at the University of Porto.

latest: mid 2021 on my website

raum.pt residency (late 2020):

I was invited to participate in an online residency by my PhD supervisor Patrícia Gouveia who then curated the residency.

Patrícia wrote a wonderful reflective text about the themes which Speak out inspired for her: raum.pt/terhi-marttila
